## Why AI Resume Builders Outperform DIY Resumes

Modern hiring has two gatekeepers: ATS software (which rejects 75% of resumes before a human sees them) and overworked recruiters (who spend 6 seconds on each resume).

AI resume builders solve both problems:

## Resume Writing Tips for 2025

**Quantify achievements:** "Increased sales by 34% in Q1" beats "Managed sales team."

**Use active verbs:** Led, Built, Launched, Reduced, Automated — not "Responsible for" or "Assisted with."

**One page under 10 years experience:** Recruiters still prefer one-page resumes for early-career candidates.

**Tailor for every application:** Adjust keywords to match the specific job description. AI builders make this fast.

**Skills section is critical:** List tools, platforms, and certifications explicitly. ATS searches for exact matches.
