## What Is JobCopilot?

JobCopilot is an AI job search automation tool that connects to your LinkedIn profile or resume, learns your job preferences, and then automatically submits applications to matching positions across the web.

Instead of spending hours scrolling job boards, you set your criteria once — job title, location, salary range, company size, industry — and JobCopilot's AI does the searching and applying for you.

## How JobCopilot Works

### Step 1: Set Up Your Profile

You start by importing your resume or connecting your LinkedIn. JobCopilot extracts your skills, experience, job titles, and education automatically. You then set your preferences:

- Target job titles (e.g., "Marketing Manager," "Software Engineer")
- Preferred locations or remote-only
- Minimum salary requirements
- Company size preferences (startup, mid-size, enterprise)
- Industries to target or exclude
- Jobs to avoid (e.g., no staffing agencies, no specific companies)

### Step 2: AI Finds and Filters Jobs

Once your profile is live, JobCopilot's AI continuously scans job postings that match your criteria. It filters out irrelevant listings, duplicates, and postings that don't meet your requirements — so you're not applying to jobs you'd never take.

### Step 3: Automatic Applications

For each matching job, the AI:
1. Fills in your application details from your profile
2. Writes a customized cover letter based on the job description
3. Submits the application on your behalf
4. Logs everything in your dashboard

You can apply to **hundreds of jobs per week** without lifting a finger.

### Step 4: Track Everything

Your dashboard shows every application in real time — when it was sent, to which company, the job title, and whether you've received any response. When a company reaches back out for an interview, you take over from there.

## What Could Be Better

### Applications Aren't Always Perfect

For complex application forms with unusual fields, the AI sometimes leaves blanks or fills incorrectly. You'll want to periodically audit your dashboard and check what's going out.

### Not a Replacement for Networking

JobCopilot handles volume, but the highest-quality job opportunities often come from referrals and networking. Think of it as handling your baseline applications while you focus your personal energy on networking.

### ATS Optimization Varies

Some Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) parse AI-submitted applications differently. For high-competition roles at large companies, a carefully hand-crafted application may still outperform an automated one. JobCopilot works best when combined with targeted manual applications for your top 10 dream companies.

### Learning Curve on Setup

Getting your preferences dialed in takes a week or two. The first round of matches may include some irrelevant jobs. Invest time upfront in setting exclusions, and the quality improves substantially.
