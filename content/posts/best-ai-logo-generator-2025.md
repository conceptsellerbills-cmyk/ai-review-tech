## What to Look for in an AI Logo

**Scalability:** Your logo must look perfect at favicon size (16x16px) and billboard size. Vector formats (SVG) are essential.

**Versatility:** Test your logo on light and dark backgrounds, in color and black-and-white. A strong logo works in all contexts.

**Distinctiveness:** Avoid logos that look like every other business in your industry. The best AI tools generate unique results — regenerate until you find something truly distinctive.

**File formats:** Ensure you receive SVG, PNG (transparent background), and ideally PDF files.
