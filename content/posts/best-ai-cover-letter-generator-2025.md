## Anatomy of a High-Converting Cover Letter

**Opening paragraph:** Hook with a specific reason you want this role at this company — not generic enthusiasm. Reference something specific about the company's mission or recent work.

**Middle paragraph(s):** Two or three specific achievements that directly address the job requirements. Use numbers: "increased conversion by 34%", "managed team of 8", "reduced churn by 20%".

**Closing paragraph:** Clear call to action. Express enthusiasm for discussing how you'd contribute, and make it easy to contact you.

**What to avoid:** Generic openers ("I am writing to apply for..."), restating your resume, overusing "I", and exceeding one page.
