# Best AI Interior Design Tools in 2025: Redesign Any Room Instantly

AI interior design tools let you upload a photo of your room and see it transformed with a completely new style, furniture arrangement, or color palette in seconds. What used to require an interior designer and rendering software is now accessible to anyone with a smartphone.

---

## 1. Reimagine Home — Best Overall

**Rating: 4.8/5**

Reimagine Home lets you upload a room photo and choose from 35+ design styles to generate photorealistic redesigns. The results are consistently impressive and accurate to the original room's dimensions and lighting.

**Key features:**
- 35+ design styles (Scandinavian, Mid-century, Industrial, etc.)
- Preserves room structure and dimensions
- Furnishing and color palette changes
- Before/after comparison
- High-resolution outputs

**Pricing:** Free (3 renders); Basic $15/month; Pro $29/month

## 2. RoomGPT — Best Free Option

**Rating: 4.6/5**

RoomGPT was one of the first viral AI interior design tools. Upload a photo, select a style, and get a redesigned room in seconds. The free tier is genuinely useful for homeowners exploring options.

**Key features:**
- Instant free room redesign
- Multiple interior styles
- Simple, minimal interface
- No account required for basic use

## Getting the Best Results from AI Interior Design

**Use well-lit, wide-angle photos.** Brighter, clearer source photos produce dramatically better redesigns. Shoot from corners to capture as much of the room as possible.

**Try multiple styles.** Generate 5-6 different style options — you may be surprised which one works best for your space.

**Use as inspiration, not a blueprint.** AI-generated interiors are excellent for direction and style choices but may suggest furniture arrangements that don't work practically. Use a professional for final planning.
