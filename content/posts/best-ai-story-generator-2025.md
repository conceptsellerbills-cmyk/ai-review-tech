## Story Generation Techniques That Work

**Give detailed character backgrounds.** The more specific your character details (backstory, motivation, flaws, speech patterns), the more consistent and interesting the AI's portrayal.

**Define your story's constraints.** What can't happen? What rules govern your world? AI stories improve dramatically when you establish clear narrative boundaries.

**Use iterative refinement.** Generate a scene, identify what's working and what isn't, then give specific instructions to improve: "The dialogue here is too formal — make it more tense and clipped."

**Maintain a story bible.** Keep a document with your characters, world rules, and plot points. Reference it in your prompts to maintain consistency.
