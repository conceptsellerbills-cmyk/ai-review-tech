# Best AI Website Builder in 2025: Build a Site Without Coding

AI website builders have made it possible to go from an idea to a fully designed, deployed website in under 10 minutes. Simply describe your business or project, and the AI generates a complete website with layout, copy, images, and colors. Here are the best options in 2025.

---

## 1. Framer AI — Best Design Quality

**Rating: 4.9/5**

Framer's AI can generate an entire website from a single text prompt with design quality that rivals hand-crafted sites. Its responsive design, animation capabilities, and CMS make it the top choice for designers and developers who want AI-assisted but fully customizable results.

**Key features:**
- Full site generation from text prompt
- Professional design quality
- Custom animations and interactions
- Built-in CMS for dynamic content
- SEO tools included
- Custom domain and hosting

**Pricing:** Free (Framer subdomain); Mini $5/month; Basic $15/month; Pro $25/month

## 2. Wix ADI — Best for Small Businesses

**Rating: 4.7/5**

Wix's AI website builder (ADI) asks a few questions about your business and creates a personalized website with relevant copy, images, and functionality. Its 900+ app marketplace and 500+ designer templates make it the most flexible option for small businesses.

**Key features:**
- Conversational setup (10-minute onboarding)
- Industry-specific templates and copy
- AI text and image generation within editor
- E-commerce, booking, and blog features built-in
- SEO wizard

**Pricing:** Free (Wix ads); Light $17/month; Core $29/month; Business $36/month

- Built-in e-commerce
- Native AI image generation
- Excellent for portfolios and creative businesses
