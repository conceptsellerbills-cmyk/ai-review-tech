## Ethical and Legal Considerations

**Always get consent.** Using someone's likeness without permission is unethical and potentially illegal in many jurisdictions.

**No non-consensual intimate imagery.** Creating fake intimate content of real people is illegal in most countries and a serious harm.

**Label synthetic content.** When sharing AI-generated face swap content, disclose that it's AI-generated, especially in social or news contexts.

**Check platform terms.** Many social platforms prohibit face swap content that isn't clearly labeled as synthetic.
