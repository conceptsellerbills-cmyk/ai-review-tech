# Best AI Meeting Tools 2025: Never Take Notes or Miss an Action Item Again

**Monthly searches: ~12,100** — The average employee spends 21.5 hours in meetings per week. AI meeting tools reclaim hours by handling transcription, summarization, and follow-up automation — so you can actually pay attention instead of typing.

## What AI Meeting Tools Do

Modern AI meeting assistants:
1. **Join your call** automatically (Zoom, Teams, Google Meet)
2. **Transcribe** the conversation in real time
3. **Identify speakers** and label their contributions
4. **Summarize** the meeting into key points
5. **Extract action items** and assign them to owners
6. **Generate follow-up emails** with decisions and next steps
7. **Search** your entire meeting history by topic

The best tools do all of this with minimal setup and sync automatically to your CRM, project management tool, or email.

## Top AI Meeting Tools Compared

### 1. Otter.ai — Best Overall Meeting AI

Otter.ai is the most widely used AI meeting tool with 20 million users. Its automatic transcription accuracy and action item extraction are best-in-class for general meetings.

**Key features:**
- Real-time transcription with 95%+ accuracy
- OtterPilot: joins Zoom, Teams, Meet automatically
- AI-generated meeting summary and action items
- Speaker identification and labeling
- Otter AI Chat: ask questions about any meeting ("what did we decide about pricing?")
- Team workspace with searchable meeting library
- Slack, Salesforce, HubSpot integrations

**Transcription accuracy:** 95%+ (English)
**Cost:** Free (300 min/month) | $16.99/month Pro

Otter's search is the killer feature for teams with extensive meeting history — finding "when did we discuss the Q2 budget?" across 200 meetings takes 2 seconds.

### 2. Fireflies.ai — Best for CRM Integration

Fireflies.ai is the choice for sales teams who need meeting data flowing automatically into Salesforce and HubSpot.

**Key features:**
- Auto-joins all calendar meetings
- CRM integration: log calls to Salesforce/HubSpot automatically
- Conversation intelligence (talk ratios, topic detection)
- AI-generated meeting summary with key metrics
- Topic tracking (flag when competitors or pricing are mentioned)
- Team analytics dashboard

**Best for:** Sales teams tracking deals across meetings
**Cost:** Free (limited) | $18/user/month Business

The CRM sync is seamless — every discovery call automatically creates a contact, logs the summary, and updates deal stage in Salesforce without any manual entry.

### 3. Fathom — Best Free Meeting Tool

Fathom is the highest-quality free AI meeting tool available. The transcription and summarization quality rival paid competitors.

**Key features:**
- Free forever (unlimited recordings for individual use)
- Real-time transcription + speaker identification
- One-click highlight during meeting
- AI summary of highlighted moments
- Action items with owner assignment
- Slack notification with meeting summary
- Zoom and Google Meet support

**Cost:** Free (individual) | $32/month (Team with CRM sync)

For individuals who want AI meeting notes without paying, Fathom is the obvious answer. The free tier is genuinely full-featured.

### 4. Gong — Best for Sales Conversations

Gong is the enterprise sales intelligence platform that analyzes every customer conversation for coaching opportunities, deal risk, and competitive intelligence.

**Key features:**
- Call recording and transcription
- AI deal risk scoring
- Competitor mention tracking
- Talk ratio and monologue analysis
- Coaching recommendations for sales managers
- Deal intelligence: correlate conversation patterns with won/lost deals

**Best for:** Enterprise B2B sales organizations
**Cost:** Enterprise pricing ($100-$200/user/year)

Gong goes beyond meeting notes — it identifies patterns across thousands of sales calls to tell you what top performers do differently.

### 5. Chorus.ai (by ZoomInfo) — Best for Enterprise Sales

Chorus serves similar customers to Gong with stronger ZoomInfo integration for research-to-call workflows.

**Key features:**

- ZoomInfo contact enrichment integration

- Buyer engagement scoring

**Best for:** Enterprise sales teams using ZoomInfo
**Cost:** Enterprise pricing

### 6. Notion AI Meeting Notes — Best for Notion Teams

If your team documents everything in Notion, the built-in AI meeting template creates structured notes directly in your workspace.

**Key features:**
- Upload transcript or paste text → AI formats into Notion structure
- Action items auto-extracted
- Decision log populated automatically
- Links to related Notion pages
- Templates for different meeting types
- Team workspace integration

**Best for:** Teams whose workflow centers on Notion
**Cost:** Included with Notion AI ($10/user/month)

### 7. tl;dv — Best for Remote Teams

tl;dv (Too Long, Didn't View) focuses on the async review use case — letting team members who couldn't attend a meeting catch up efficiently.

**Key features:**
- Record Zoom and Google Meet with AI notes
- Highlight reel creation (share only the important minutes)
- AI meeting reports (auto-distributed to stakeholders)
- Timestamp-linked clips for easy reference
- CRM integrations
- Free tier with unlimited recordings

**Best for:** Distributed remote teams with async workflows
**Cost:** Free | $18/user/month Pro

The highlight reel feature changes how meeting information spreads through an organization — instead of a 60-minute recording, share a 5-minute reel of decisions.

### 8. Avoma — Best for Mid-Market Sales Teams

Avoma covers the full meeting lifecycle: scheduling, preparation, notes, and follow-up — with strong collaboration features for teams.

**Key features:**
- Pre-meeting agenda with AI-suggested talking points
- Real-time transcription with live bookmarking
- Auto-generated meeting notes by agenda section
- CRM integration with deal stage tracking
- Revenue intelligence analytics
- Team coaching scorecard

**Best for:** Inside sales and CS teams in the 20-200 employee range
**Cost:** $19/user/month (starter) | $59/user/month (revenue intelligence)

Avoma's pre-meeting preparation (AI-generated talking points based on CRM history) is a feature most competitors lack.

## Getting the Most from AI Meeting Tools

**Set up speaker profiles:** Most tools need a voice sample to accurately identify speakers. Do this in the first meeting.

**Review action items before logging off:** AI-generated action items are 85% accurate. A 60-second review prevents the 15% from becoming dropped commitments.

**Use the search feature actively:** The meeting library is only valuable if you use it. Build the habit of searching before starting a new discussion.

**Share summaries immediately:** Send the AI-generated summary to all attendees right after the meeting while context is fresh for any corrections.
